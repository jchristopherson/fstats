Returns
The value of the function.
Remarks
The CDF for a Chi-squared distribution is given as \( F(x) = \frac{ \gamma \left( \frac{k}{2}, \frac{x}{2} \right) } { \Gamma \left( \frac{k}{2} \right)} \) where \( \gamma \) is the lower incomplete gamma function, and \( k \) is the number of degrees of freedom.
